As a small business owner, you're likely struggling to manage multiple marketing tasks, from email campaigns to social media posts, while trying to grow your customer base. Marketing automation can be a huge help, but with so many tools available, it's hard to choose the right one. In this article, we'll compare two popular options: n8n and Zapier, to help you decide which one is best for your business.

Both n8n and Zapier are powerful automation tools that can help you streamline your marketing workflows. However, they have different strengths and weaknesses, and the right choice for you will depend on your specific needs and budget.

### Pricing

One of the main differences between n8n and Zapier is pricing. As of 2026, n8n offers a free plan with limited features, as well as several paid plans starting at $25 per month. Zapier, on the other hand, offers a free trial, but its paid plans start at $19.99 per month.

Pro tip: If you're just starting out with marketing automation, consider starting with n8n's free plan to get a feel for the tool and see if it's right for you. [Try the tool free](#) and explore its features before committing to a paid plan.

### Features

Both n8n and Zapier offer a wide range of features, including workflow automation, API connections, and data mapping. However, n8n is known for its flexibility and customizability, with a wide range of nodes and functions that can be used to create complex workflows. Zapier, on the other hand, is more user-friendly and has a larger library of pre-built integrations.

Pro tip: If you have a lot of custom workflow requirements, n8n may be the better choice. However, if you're looking for a more user-friendly interface and don't need as much customization, Zapier may be the way to go.

### Ease of use

Both n8n and Zapier are relatively easy to use, but Zapier has a more user-friendly interface and is generally easier to navigate. n8n, on the other hand, has a steeper learning curve, but offers more advanced features and customization options.

### Support

Both n8n and Zapier offer support through email and community forums. However, Zapier also offers phone support, which can be helpful if you need more personalized assistance.

### Winner per use case

Based on our comparison, here are the winners per use case:

* **Small to medium-sized businesses**: Zapier is the better choice due to its user-friendly interface and more affordable pricing.
